Made with Unity
3 person team

What I worked on:
I was responsible for creating the initial level design plans and mood boards. After this, I created first blockout of this level, which pulled from a pool of mechanics that we had programmed in advance.

After the initial blockout had been completed, I paired up with two other people to refine the level. We focused on the narrative the player would experience, refining the critical path, and building a cohesive space.

While most of our models are from Sketchfab, I additionally modelled the main temple in the level using .

Download the game on itch.io:

Derelict Spire is a vertical slice of a larger exploration game. It tutorializes a new mechanic - a grappling hook - to the player, while reinforcing the mechanics they had previously learned (collecting artifacts and destroying terrain that blocks their path).
