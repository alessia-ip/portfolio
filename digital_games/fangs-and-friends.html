Fangs and Friends | 2019

Made with Unity
7 person team | 10 weeks

What I worked on:
Officially, I was the team’s producer, as well as lead level designer and lead UI designer. I also played the role of a technical narrative designer, as I designed and developed easy to implement dialogue systems to support quick dialogue implementation for our narrative designer.

As a whole however, most members of the team, including myself, worked on every role in small amounts to prevent bottlenecks and tackle difficult problems that arose during development.

Fangs and Friends is an award winning, co-op puzzle game focused on short, asynchronous gameplay. Players work together with their different abilities to solve puzzles and escape!
