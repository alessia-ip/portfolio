Skywalker's Six: Six Degrees of Separation In Star Wars

This visualization was created to explore alternative modes of visualization, and what better set of connections to use than the cast of Star Wars to set the scene?

Wookieepedia
Any information gathered for this project outside of my geeky knowledge was obtained from here!


This project was completed using Adobe Illustrator - a grid was used to line up all the names in a spoke pattern around the central point of Anakin's name. From here, the spokes go outward, with each character being connected to their connections as well. Connection types are specified in the legend of the work.
