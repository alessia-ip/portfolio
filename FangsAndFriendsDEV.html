Level Design

These were the rough level designs I created, which we based our initial greyboxes off of. These were later reworked into the current levels. Only the tutorial level has remained fairly static out of all the puzzles we created, however these served as the first tests for all of the mechanics we had, to deem whether or not they were fun, and how balanced they were as well.
