Novum - A Summary

Novum, named in homage of Darko Suvin’s writing on science fiction, is a short, interactive, multi-user, sci-fi augmented reality experience. Referencing the world-building done in ‘The Aliens’, a story by Murray Leinster, Novum explores a brief narrative that still holds relevance today in topics of xenophobia, bias, hiring practices, and personal feelings of exclusion and non-conformity.
