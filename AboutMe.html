About Me!

Hi, my name is Alessia Ianni-Palarchio, and I’m a fourth year student at OCAD University, in the Digital Futures program. It’s here that I’ve been able to combine my love for design, and creative endeavours, with a passion for the digital. It’s given me a space to create a union between these two areas that I love, since design thinking marries them perfectly together.

My current body of work focuses mainly on digital games, augmented reality, and virtual reality. That said, my skills branch out into the areas of data visualization, design, and physical fabrications, which often all come together when dealing with mediums that bridge the gap between the physical and the digital (such as with AR).

Currently I’m finishing my undergrad thesis on the future of entertainment media, specifically with the rising popularity and availability of alternate reality technology, and how this looks to shape science-fiction, group oriented entertainment in the near future. Concurrently, I’m also the project lead of a group of seven (including myself) making a game to show at this year’s Level Up showcase.

Imagine the future!
