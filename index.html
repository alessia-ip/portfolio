Alessia Ianni-Palarchio is a maker, currently studying at OCAD University in the Digital Futures program. She always has a new project on the go! Her main body of work focuses on the intersection between the technical and the creative, situated in digital design and interesting technology. Recently, much of this resides within the spectrum of AR, VR, and digital games.
