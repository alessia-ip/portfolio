The Derelict Spire (2021)

The Derelict Spire is a blockout of a level that is inspired by the San Simeone Piccolo, in Venice, and the coastline of Tropea, Calabria. This level is a small slice of a game that tutorializes a new mechanic for the player, while reinforcing their currently learned mechanics.

This project is currently being expanded upon further now that the greybox is completed, and will be refined with an art pass and lighting now that the concept has been solidified.

Novum (2019)

Novum, named in homage of Darko Suvin’s writing on science fiction, was a short, interactive, multi-user, augmented reality experience. Referencing ‘The Aliens’, a sci-fi story by Murray Leinster, Novum is a two person experience that explores a brief narrative between the participants. It brings forth topics of xenophobia, bias, and personal feelings of exclusion and non-conformity, utilizing the capabilities that augmented reality brings to the table to create a rich experience.

Novum strove to answer the question “what impact will participatory, group oriented science-fiction experiences have on entertainment media?”

Data Viz (2017)

I had an opportunity to write a short article about two data visualization pieces I had made, and why they worked. This was a very brief article that was a glance at the process that goes into data visualization work, and that extended into the UI/UX related work that I did for clients at the company I wrote it for. It was a starting point in explaining that data tells a story, and it is up to the person making the visualization (interactive or otherwise) to ensure that they were choosing the right way to tell that story in a manner that their audience will understand.
